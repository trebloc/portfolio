Impacts of the ECB Announcement
The ECB Announcement can have an immense impact on bonds, currencies, and equities through changes to the interest rates and the use of quantitative easing.
  • Rising interest rates or decreasing quantitative easing, markets tend to slowdown creating a bearish market.
  • Low interest rates and an increase in quantitative easing are the signals of a bullish market.
  • In the wake of the ECB Announcement, the subsequent press conference creates another trading moment.
